Southern Region I Highlights

The region offers many ways for large and small Water/Wastewater Agencies to find assistance during emergency response as well as get involved in helping to prepare the region for local emergencies and major disasters.

WEROC

The purpose of the Water Emergency Response Organization of Orange County (WEROC) is to coordinate and support an effective emergency response to a major disaster on behalf of all Orange County water agencies. To carry out this mission WEROC provides services that promote planning and preparedness activities for both the agencies, as well as its own Emergency Operations Center volunteer staff. The program also takes an active role in maintaining two EOCs and emergency response equipment for use during a disaster.
